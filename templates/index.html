FAQ

What is blind judging?
Options are randomly shuffled and anonymized before any AI judge sees them. No judge knows which option is A, B, or C. This eliminates position bias and ensures fair evaluation.
How many judges evaluate my decision?
Up to 3 independent AI judges (GPT-4o, Claude, Gemini) evaluate every decision. Their scores are aggregated using a rank-point consensus system. Judge agreement is reported in the confidence score.
Is it free?
Yes. DecideIQ is free during the public beta. No signup required. Just enter your decision and get results.
Can I share results?
Every analysis generates a unique shareable link. Send it to colleagues, clients, or friends. The result page includes full scores, rankings, and judge consensus data.
