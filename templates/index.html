FAQ

What is blind judging?
Options are randomly shuffled and anonymized. No judge knows which option is A, B, or C. This eliminates position bias.
How many judges?
Up to 3 independent AI judges (GPT-4o, Claude, Gemini). Scores aggregated with rank-point consensus.
Is it free?
Yes. Free during the public beta. No signup required.
